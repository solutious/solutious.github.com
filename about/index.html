A Note From the Founder

I started this company in 2006 as a technical consultancy to help clients improve development processes, build production environments, and prepare for launch-day. I've worked hard for small companies like ApproxiMatch and Status.net and large companies like Amdocs and Yellow Pages Group. I had a great time, met a lot of awesome people, and learned a ton.

But there's a blight in the software industry that I'm determined to do something about: overly complex technology that wastes people's time. Technology should reduce overall complexity, yet I have found case after case where the opposite is true. Where's the love?

So in 2008 I started focusing my attention on building great open source tools. I won't claim that our software is perfect, but it's built with care and attention down to even the smallest detail. I'm absolutely committed to ensuring it provides a net benefit to you and your projects. If you have any questions, problems, suggestions, or any other issue with any Solutious products, don't hesitate to contact me.
