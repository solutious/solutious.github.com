About Solutious.

Solutious is a software testing company. We are building next-generation tools to help businesses ensure their applications are ready to serve their customers on launch day. Established in 2006 as a technical consultancy, we helped clients build web applications, design and maintain production environments, and prepare application infrastructures for release readiness. Now we're building our knowledge and experience into the testing tools we've always wanted.

The old guard build complex products so they can charge for training and professional services. That way, you're locked in with proprietary formats and licensing agreements. We believe that B2B technology should reduce complexity and open up access to data. It shouldn't require extensive training and gigantic manuals - it should provide value from Day One. Further, it shouldn't keep you from your data; it should create new ways to help you visualize and understand it but also allow you access to the full, "raw" data set. We also believe that professional services shouldn't be a required part of the process, but we're here if you need us.

We recently released a preview version of one of our projects, named Stella. It's a command-line tool for executing, investigating, and reporting on performance tests. You can check it out on Github and Rubyforge.
